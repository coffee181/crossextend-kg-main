### Product Description

The CATL battery_product_001 is a state-of-the-art Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) prismatic cell engineered for high-energy density applications requiring exceptional longevity and inherent safety. Utilizing CATL's proprietary cell-to-pack (CTP) and cell chemistry optimization technologies, this cell delivers a robust 202Ah capacity within a compact, mechanically stable aluminum casing. The LFP cathode chemistry provides outstanding thermal and chemical stability, significantly reducing risks associated with thermal runaway compared to other lithium-ion chemistries.

This cell is designed with a focus on total cost of ownership (TCO), offering a cycle life exceeding 6,000 cycles, which translates to extended service life in demanding daily cycling applications. Its wide operating temperature tolerance ensures reliable performance in diverse environmental conditions. The cell incorporates advanced electrode design and electrolyte formulation to minimize internal resistance, enhancing both energy efficiency and power delivery consistency. It is intended as a core building block for battery pack manufacturers, providing a reliable, long-lasting, and safe energy storage solution that is scalable for systems ranging from tens of kilowatt-hours to multi-megawatt-hour installations.

### Key Usage Scenarios

1.  **Commercial & Utility-Scale Energy Storage Systems (ESS):** Ideal for grid-level peak shaving, frequency regulation, and renewable energy integration (solar/wind) due to its long cycle life, high safety, and excellent calendar life.
2.  **Electric Commercial Vehicles (ECVs):** Specifically suited for electric buses, logistics vehicles, and medium-duty trucks where safety, durability, and total cost of ownership are paramount over extreme energy density.
3.  **Telecommunications & Backup Power (UPS):** Provides reliable, maintenance-friendly backup power for telecom towers and critical data center infrastructure, with stable performance over a wide temperature range.

### Safety Features

*   **Inherently Stable Chemistry:** The strong P-O bond in the LiFePO₄ cathode provides high thermal stability, resisting oxygen release at high temperatures.
*   **Advanced Venting Mechanism:** Integrated safety vent designed to relieve internal pressure in extreme fault conditions, preventing rupture.
*   **Ceramic-Coated Separator:** Features a heat-resistant separator with ceramic coating to mitigate internal short-circuit risks.
*   **Sturdy Prismatic Housing:** Robust aluminum casing provides mechanical integrity and efficient heat dissipation.
*   **Stable Solid Electrolyte Interface (SEI):** Optimized electrolyte formulation promotes the formation of a stable SEI layer, reducing gassing and enhancing cycle life.
