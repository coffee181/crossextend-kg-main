| T8 | Run coolant through the repaired inlet branch and observe the connector while the pump cycles and the branch warms, because some replacement connectors stay dry at rest but sweat once the hose relaxes into its loaded position. Record both flow condition and surface state during this dynamic check. |
| T9 | Reinstall the access panels and repeat the same operating state that originally produced the leak so the final judgment reflects the guarded configuration and the real support path from connector to clip. The restored branch has to survive shield preload as well as coolant flow. |
