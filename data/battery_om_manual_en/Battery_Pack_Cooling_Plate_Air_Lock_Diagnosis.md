|---|---|
| T1 | If Trion PackTherm-530 LR shows a cold-side derate after coolant service, record the pump command, inlet NTC, outlet NTC, reservoir level, and whether the branch was recently drained or opened at the pack high point. Keep the temperature readings tied to the same service mode that produced the complaint. |
| T2 | Expose the pack high-point bleed port, supply hose, return hose, clear bleed line if fitted, reservoir neck, and the nearest cold-plate section before venting anything, then compare hose temperature from inlet to outlet with the pump still off. A sharp cold zone downstream of the bleed point is often the first sign that trapped gas sits inside the plate. |
| T3 | Confirm reservoir level and pump activity first, then open the designated bleed point slightly and observe whether solid coolant appears or whether foam and intermittent bubbles emerge. Record the sequence of bubbles, the time to stable flow, and the change in hose firmness so the trapped-air diagnosis rests on direct evidence. |
| T4 | Command the pump as allowed by service mode and compare inlet-to-outlet temperature spread before and after bleeding while the branch remains in the same operating state. If temperature begins to equalize only after air clears, keep the boundary at the pack high point; if no thermal change occurs, keep the fault upstream on reservoir fill or pump operation instead. |
| T5 | Restore the bleed point and repeat the same thermal demand long enough to show that the cold plate no longer develops the original cold pocket or delayed warm-up. The branch is not cleared if the derate message disappears briefly and then returns on the next pump cycle. |
| T6 | Close the diagnosis with the exact root boundary, such as trapped air at the high-point bleed, low reservoir after service, or absent pump action, and include the before and after temperature behavior beside that component. The accepted state is a branch with continuous coolant discharge at bleed, stable hose temperatures from inlet to outlet, and no renewed cold-side derate in the repeated mode. |
