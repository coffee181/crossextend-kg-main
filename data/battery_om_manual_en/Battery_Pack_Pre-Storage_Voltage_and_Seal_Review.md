| T5 | Check traceability labels, hazard markings, and shelf-position tags, then confirm they remain readable without removing wraps or moving the pack from its assigned pallet orientation. Correct labeling is part of technical control because it avoids unnecessary handling and connector disturbance during storage checks. |
| T6 | Reinspect seal edges, restraint straps, vent clearances, and capped interfaces after final wrapping and strapping because some storage faults appear only after full compression and transport preload are applied. This reread ties acceptance to the actual stored configuration rather than to a partially wrapped condition. |
