| T8 | Record the checked terminal identity, measured before and after voltages, thermal evidence, and final lug condition in one traceable note. The report should identify exactly which secondary joint at T1-X2 was serviced and how the loaded voltage changed. |
| T9 | Close the work only after the report names the exact failed boundary, such as one loose secondary lug, oxidized contact surface, washer-order issue, or cable side load, and ties it to measured voltage drop and thermal behavior.
